## 2. Build vs. Buy vs. Rent Decision

### Option A: Build It (Your Current Direction)
**Investment:**
- 12-16 weeks development time (solo)
- $3K-10K first year (proxies, hosting, captcha)
- Ongoing maintenance (site updates break bots)

**Pros:**
- Own the bot, no recurring rental fees
- Customize for your specific needs
- Potential to resell as service
- Learning experience

**Cons:**
- High upfront time investment
- Anti-bot bypass is genuinely difficult
- May take 6-12 months to break even
- "sometimes the bot completely fails" - Discord

**Recommended if:**
- You have 10+ hours/week for 3+ months
- Technical challenge excites you
- Long-term view (12+ months)
- Want to offer service to community

### Option B: Rent Stellar AIO ($39/day)
**Investment:**
- $39 × 10 drop days = $390/month
- Still need proxies ($200-500/month)
- Total: ~$600-900/month

**Pros:**
- Immediate start
- Proven reliability (1M+ checkouts)
- No development time
- Learn the ecosystem first

**Cons:**
- Recurring cost eats into profits
- Can't resell as service
- No ownership

**Recommended if:**
- Want to test profitability first
- Need results in next 30 days
- Unsure about building commitment

### Option C: Buy Existing Bot ($5K+)
**Investment:**
- $5,000-10,000 upfront
- Still need proxies ($200-500/month)

**Pros:**
- Immediate ownership
- Proven reliability
- Can resell as service
- No development time

**Cons:**
- High upfront cost
- May not fit your exact needs
- Still need to learn to use it

**Recommended if:**
- Have capital available
- Want ownership without building
- Ready to scale quickly

### Option D: Hybrid Approach (Recommended)
**Month 1-2: Rent Stellar AIO ($78)**
- Test ecosystem, learn workflow
- Validate profitability on 3-5 drops
- Understand anti-bot challenges
- Document what works/doesn't

**Month 3+: Build or Buy Decision**
- If profitable: Build custom bot
- If not profitable: Don't invest more
- If highly profitable: Consider buying existing bot

## 12. Key Insights from Discord Conversation

> "knowing how to make a good bot that can pass securities, thats why people pay thousands for keys"

**Translation:** The hard part isn't clicking "checkout" - it's bypassing Cloudflare, PerimeterX, DataDome, etc. This requires deep technical knowledge.

> "cheap proxies can fail and can fuck up ur whole drop"

**Translation:** Proxies are non-negotiable expense. Budget $200-500/month minimum.

> "you are still competing with other people"

**Translation:** Bot gives you advantage, but doesn't guarantee success. Expect 40-60% hit rate.

> "its not worth it for my small scale"

**Translation:** Need either (1) high-margin products or (2) scale to make money. Individual flipping may not justify bot investment.

> "the money is in both" (reselling items + offering service)

**Translation:** Maximize profitability by using bot yourself AND selling ACO services to others.
